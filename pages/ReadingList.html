Wisdom of the Leaves
...until
He that dwelleth in the secret place of the Most High, shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty.
He shall say of the Lord: "You are my refuge and my fortress, you are my God in whom I trust!"

For surely the Lord will deliver you from the snare of the enemy and from deadly pestilence...

He will cover you with his wings, and under their protection you will be safe; his faithfulness will be your defence and your shield.

For you shall not be afraid of the terror that comes by night, or the arrow that flies by day, or the plague that strikes in the dark, or the pestilence that wasteth at noonday.

A thousand may fall by your left, ten thousand by your right, but no harm will befall you.

And with your eyes you shall see the punishment of the wicked...

...because you have made the Lord your refuge, and the Most High is your habitation: There no disaster will come to you, and neither shall any evil come near your household.

For God will give his angels charge over you, to protect you in all your comings and goings...

They will hold you up in their hands to keep your feet from striking a stone.

You will trample upon the lion and the adder; upon ferocious beasts and poisonous snakes. The lion and the Dragon shall you trample under your feet!

Because he has set his faith upon the Lord, the Most High will deliver him

God says: "I will save those who love me, and I will protect those who acknowledge me as Lord."

I will set him on high because he hath known my name

When he calls upon me I will answer him... i will be with him in times of trouble.

With long life i will reward him, and I will show him my salvation!
